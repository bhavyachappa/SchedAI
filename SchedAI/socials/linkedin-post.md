# LinkedIn Post — SchedAI

## Final Draft

I used to hate adding events to Google Calendar manually.

Every time I had a class, meeting, or deadline — open the app, tap the date, fill the title, set the time, add location, pick reminders. Repeat for every single thing.

So I'd just dump everything into my WhatsApp personal chat instead. Worked until I lost it all in a scroll.

I also love seeing my schedule laid out cleanly in calendar. So I figured — I have the tools, I have the time, why not just build something that connects the two?

That's SchedAI.

Drop a message in WhatsApp → it shows up in Google Calendar. Voice note? Transcribed and scheduled. Five events in one message? All of them. Even saves ideas with auto-categories. Made a mistake? Reply UNDO to the confirmation and it's gone.

And I was strict about one thing: it had to cost close to nothing.

The whole stack runs on free tiers — Groq (Llama 3.3 + Whisper), n8n Cloud, Twilio sandbox, Google Calendar API.

Monthly cost: ~$0.

Built it for myself. But if enough people find this useful, I can write up exactly how I built it — the n8n workflows, the AI prompts, the whole thing.
